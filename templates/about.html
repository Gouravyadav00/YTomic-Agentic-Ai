🎬 YTomic — AI-Powered YouTube Clip Extractor 🚀

Made with Flask Powered by OpenAI Version 1.0.0

YTomic transforms lengthy YouTube videos into impactful short-form content. Using advanced AI, we identify and extract the most compelling moments, creating ready-to-share clips that capture attention and drive engagement. Perfect for content creators, marketers, and social media enthusiasts. 📈🎞️

🎯 Core Features

  • 🔗 Universal YouTube URL Support
  • 🧠 AI-Powered Content Analysis
  • ✂️ Smart Clip Extraction (8-10 highlights)
  • 📝 SEO-Optimized Metadata Generation
  • 📤 One-Click YouTube Upload
  • 🎨 Auto-Captioning & Visual Editing

⚡ How It Works

  1. Paste your YouTube URL
  2. AI analyzes the transcript
  3. Key moments are identified
  4. Clips are automatically trimmed
  5. Metadata is generated
  6. Ready to share or upload!
